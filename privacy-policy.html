Privacy Policy

At the Interactive Image Gallery, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our website.

We collect personal data when you interact with the gallery, such as when you search for images, select categories, or view full-screen images. This information is used to improve the gallery experience and provide personalized content.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to enhance your experience on our site, provide customer support, and notify you of updates and new features related to the gallery.

3. Data Protection

We implement various security measures to ensure your personal information is protected. We use encryption and other security technologies to safeguard your data.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to improve site performance and understand user behavior. By using our website, you agree to the use of cookies as outlined in this policy.
